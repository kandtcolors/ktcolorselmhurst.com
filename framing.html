Framing

Whether your wish to frame an original painting, an antique etching, a family portrait, a prized photo or street art from your last vacation, we can create a design for you and your home. We can enhance stitchery of all sorts, children’s art, grandma’s lace handkerchief, or dad’s favorite fishing lure. Diplomas, awards and wedding invitations deserve special framing.

Shadow boxes of all sizes and styles can be created to highlight memorabilia, medals, sports jerseys, and brass plates. We can frame a christening gown, record-breaking track shoes, grandpa’s pocket watch, or a horse bridle.

We can repair and retouch damaged canvases as well as clean and conserve art that needs care due to age or poor storage conditions.

Old, cherished art deserves the gift of newer protective matting and glass. New technology has produced ways to preserve your art.

Whether a small family treasure, a personal gallery, an office or an entire home, we can design a solution for your artwork. 25 years of experience have given us the ability to meet every framing possibility with a creative design to please you.
